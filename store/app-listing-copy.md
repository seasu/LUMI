## 完整描述

### 繁體中文版（App Store / Play Store）

**Lumi — 你的 AI 智慧衣櫥助理**

你有多少次買了「感覺很熟悉」的衣服，回家才發現衣櫃裡早就有一件幾乎一樣的？Lumi 讓這件事成為過去式。

**📷 一拍即入櫥**
拍下衣物照片，Lumi AI 自動辨識類型、顏色與材質，幫你建立完整的數位衣櫥——不需要手動輸入，不需要分類整理。

**🔍 Lumi-Check：購物前先查重**
逛街看到喜歡的衣服？開啟 Lumi-Check，拍下或選取商品圖，AI 立刻比對你的整個衣櫥，找出相似的既有衣物——讓你更聰明地消費，少買重複的衣服。

**📱 完全在你的裝置上**
所有衣物照片與 AI 分析結果都儲存在你的裝置本機，不上傳至雲端，完整保護你的隱私。

**✨ 為什麼選擇 Lumi？**
- 一鍵建立數位衣櫥，無需手動分類
- AI 自動辨識衣物類型與顏色
- 購物前比對，避免重複消費
- 照片存本機，隱私有保障
- 簡潔直覺的介面，隨時管理衣物

立刻下載 Lumi，讓你的衣櫥更聰明、購物更明智。

### English Version (App Store / Play Store)

**Lumi — Your AI-Powered Smart Wardrobe**

Ever bought a piece of clothing only to get home and realize you already own something almost identical? Lumi makes sure that never happens again.

**📷 Snap to Add**
Take a photo of any clothing item and Lumi's AI automatically recognizes its type, color, and material — building your complete digital wardrobe without any manual input or sorting.

**🔍 Lumi-Check: Shop Smarter**
Spotted something you like while shopping? Launch Lumi-Check, snap or upload a photo of the item, and AI instantly searches your entire wardrobe for similar pieces — helping you shop smarter and avoid duplicate purchases.

**📱 Everything Stays on Your Device**
All clothing photos and AI analysis results are stored locally on your device, never uploaded to the cloud — your privacy is fully protected.

**✨ Why Lumi?**
- Build your digital wardrobe in seconds, no manual sorting needed
- AI auto-detects clothing type, color, and fabric
- Compare before you buy to avoid duplicates
- Photos stay on your device for complete privacy
- Clean, intuitive interface for effortless wardrobe management

Download Lumi now and turn your wardrobe into your smartest shopping assistant.
